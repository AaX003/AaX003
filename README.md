### About Me  
My software development journey started during college. Initially, I used to *hate* coding because I thought I wasn't good at it. I often compared my programs with others and convinced myself I wasn't good enough. However, my perspective started to shift after realizing my code didn't have to be perfect, not when I was just starting out. When striving to become better at something, you should never aim for **perfection**, but **progress**. As of now, I'm constantly experimenting, creating, and learning, and I hope my journey inspires other new developers to take that first step like how I did!
